Dumb Money Clips Poster

A private tool that publishes short highlight clips to our own @dumbmoneyclips social accounts.

What it does

The app uploads short vertical video clips — reposted with permission from Dumb Money Hunter's authorized livestreams — to our own TikTok, YouTube, and other channels. Every clip credits and tags the original creator. The app claims no ownership of the source content and is not offered to the public as a service.

How it connects

It authorizes through each platform's official login (OAuth). No passwords are stored; access can be revoked at any time from the platform's connected-apps settings.
